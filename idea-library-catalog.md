# GrowthX Voice AI Buildathon Idea Library

Source: https://growthx.club/docs/handbook-ringgai-growthx

Extracted with Firecrawl CLI. 30 ideas across 5 categories.

## Growth & GTM

Activation, conversion, reactivation — revenue leaking at the edges of your funnel.

### 1. New signups who finished KYC but never invested

**Moves:** Activation rate

KYC done, bank linked, then silence. The gap between onboarding and the first trade is where AUM dies.

### 2. Merchants who integrated the gateway but never went live

**Moves:** Merchant activation

Gateway integration complete, zero live transactions. The hard part is done — something stopped them at the last mile.

### 3. Customers who bought once and never came back

**Moves:** Repeat purchase rate

They liked you once. Bring them back before a rival does.

### 4. Prospects who went silent after the demo

**Moves:** Pipeline conversion

High intent signals everywhere, zero response to the AE. A well-timed voice call converts what email never will.

### 5. Investors with money on the platform who stopped logging in

**Moves:** AUM reactivation

The money is already there. No login in 90 days. A check-in call resurfaces the portfolio.

### 6. Trial users in the final 72 hours

**Moves:** Trial-to-paid

They love the product but have not pulled the trigger. Budget, approval, or a missing feature?

### 7. Subscribers whose payment failed

**Moves:** Involuntary churn

The customer never decided to leave — their card did. One call saves the subscription.

### 8. Calling inbound demo requests back within minutes

**Moves:** Lead-to-meeting conversion

Conversion drops by the minute after the form fill. Call back fast, qualify, and book the meeting.

### 9. Personal-loan leads from paid ads that mostly turn out junk

**Moves:** Cost per qualified lead

Meta and Google fill the form for pennies, but most leads are wrong-income, rate-shoppers or mis-taps. The team burns the day on dead numbers while the pixel keeps buying more of the same.

## Founders & Sales

Renewals, churn, competitive losses — the conversations that decide your ARR.

### 10. Exit interviews within 48 hours of cancellation

**Moves:** Churn reduction

Wait a week and you get polite nothing. A structured call within 48 hours gets the honest answer.

### 11. Accounts that just downgraded

**Moves:** Revenue retention

Not churn — negotiation. What made them shrink, and what would make them grow back?

### 12. Overdue invoices stuck in someone’s finance queue

**Moves:** Days sales outstanding

Day 45, unpaid — not refusal, just stuck with someone's finance team. Find where it is stuck, get a date.

### 13. Candidates who accepted the offer but might not join

**Moves:** Offer-to-join rate

30–50% of accepted offers never show up on day one. Keep-warm calls catch counter-offers early.

## Marketplace, Fintech & Mobility Operators

Sellers, merchants, drivers, collections — the operational pulse of the platform.

### 14. Sellers who registered but never listed a product

**Moves:** Seller activation

Registered, KYC uploaded, zero listings. Every day without a catalog is GMV you will never see.

### 15. Investors whose SIPs stopped

**Moves:** Recurring AUM

Market fear, cash crunch, or just forgot? Every silent month is compounding AUM loss.

### 16. Drivers whose weekly trips collapsed

**Moves:** Supply retention

Earnings, incentives, app issue, or a rival platform? You need to know before they are gone.

### 17. Collection calls for overdue EMIs

**Moves:** Collections rate

Cash flow, dispute, or forgot? The first call decides the entire recovery strategy.

### 18. Merchants whose payment volume suddenly dropped

**Moves:** Revenue recovery

No ticket, no complaint, no churn request. Just silence. Nobody investigated.

### 19. Fraud verification calls for flagged transactions

**Moves:** Fraud loss ratio

The system flagged it. A call within minutes verifies identity, confirms or denies, and unblocks the legitimate ones.

### 20. Loan applications stuck on one missing document

**Moves:** Application-to-disbursal rate

Pre-approved, then stalled on one missing document. An incomplete file is revenue sitting one PDF away.

### 21. Re-KYC before the account freezes

**Moves:** Re-KYC completion rate

Miss the RBI window and the account freezes. Every freeze is a support ticket plus a churn risk.

## Healthcare Ops

The patient left the building. The care did not end.

### 22. Checking patients are taking their medicines after discharge

**Moves:** Readmission rate

Side effects, cost, confusion, or no family support — catch non-adherence before it becomes a readmission.

### 23. Patients who never booked the follow-up appointment

**Moves:** Clinic revenue

The treatment is incomplete and the revenue is leaking. Capture the reason and rebook on the spot.

### 24. Confirming patients are ready 48 hours before surgery

**Moves:** OT utilization

Wrong prep means a cancelled procedure and a wasted OT slot. Confirm readiness 48 hours before.

### 25. Chronic patients who stopped refilling their medicines

**Moves:** Medication adherence

Switched pharmacies — or stopped taking the medication? For diabetes and hypertension, the difference is everything.

## Product Managers

Adoption, retention signals, pricing, research — the problems you live in every sprint.

### 26. Accounts whose usage is falling with zero complaints

**Moves:** Logo retention

No complaints, no tickets, just declining logins. A renewal nobody knows is at risk yet.

### 27. Calling unhappy customers after an NPS drop

**Moves:** NPS recovery

Survey data gives you the score, not the story. Run follow-up conversations with the detractors.

### 28. Feature requests you're about to build without validating

**Moves:** Roadmap hit rate

The same request keeps coming up, so it's near the top of the roadmap. But nobody has called the people who asked to find what they're really trying to do — and half of built features ship to silence.

### 29. Pricing changes shipped on gut instead of what customers will pay

**Moves:** Willingness to pay

The price is about to move — on instinct, a board nudge, and a glance at a competitor. Nobody has asked customers what they'd actually pay, or what makes them flinch, before a number that touches everyone goes live.

### 30. Users who stalled in onboarding and never reached first value

**Moves:** Activation rate

They signed up, started setup, and stopped one step short of the aha moment. The dashboards show the drop-off cliff but never the reason — and that reason is the highest-leverage thing a PM can learn.
