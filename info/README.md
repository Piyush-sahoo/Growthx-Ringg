## Event at a glance

- **Event:** GrowthX Voice AI Buildathon (Bengaluru)
- **Date:** June 14 (10:00 AM – 6:00 PM)
- **Constraint:** You **must** build on **Ringg AI** (voice orchestration layer). Everything else (LLM, backend, frontend) is your choice.
- **Format:** Solo or teams, on-site only, one submission per team.
- **Prizes:** ₹2,00,000 (1st) · ₹1,00,000 (2nd) · ₹50,000 (3rd) — total ₹3.5 lakhs.
