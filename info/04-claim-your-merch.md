# 04 / Claim Your Merch

**Claim your merch. Build in public, or it's just a demo.**

Ship on Ringg, post it to the world, show us the post, and walk away with merch.

## Build in public

> "A product with no users and no GTM is just a demo."

The build is half the work. The other half is putting it in front of people. The
builders who ship in public — who show what they made, what broke, and what they
learned — are the ones building products, not demos.

## The drop

- Tote bag with "BUILD TALK SHIP VOICE AI"
- Stickers
- T-shirt
- Keychain

## How to claim

1. **Build on the Ringg AI platform.** A voice agent you actually made at the buildathon.
2. **Post it on socials.** Build in public — share photos, a video or screen recording,
   or just what you built and how the buildathon went. Tag GrowthX and Ringg AI.
3. **Show us the post.** Find any GrowthX team member and pull up the live post on your phone.
4. **We verify it, you grab your merch.** A team member confirms the post is live, and the merch is yours.

> **First come, first served.** The merch is limited — once it's gone, it's gone.
